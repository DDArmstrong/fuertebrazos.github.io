Article Pitch

Employees and customers in love

A data-driven story showing that companies with happy customers and happy employees grow faster. It centers on a 2x2 scatterplot that maps employee satisfaction against customer satisfaction at companies ranging from Trader Joe’s to Chick fil-A and Fedex.

The example shows supermarkets. I have charts for sectors ranging from fast food to package delivery (US Post Office, anyone?). All tell a similar story. Which companies are most loved, least loved and getting the biggest financial payoffs is often a surprise

Methodology:

Methodology

I pulled employee satisfaction ratings from Glassdoor using a scraper built with Python’s BeautifulSoup library.

The customer satisfaction measure came from the American Customer Satisfaction Index.

Bubbles were sized to reflect 3-year revenue growth figures from IEX Cloud API, pulled using a Python script.

Chart was constructed in Datawrapper.
