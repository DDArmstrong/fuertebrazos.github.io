Happiness is contagious

Employees and customers in love

By Dan Armstrong

Skills: Website scraping (Python, BeautifulSoup). Financial APIs (IEX Cloud). Datawrapper.

I wanted to investigate the correlation between employee and customer satisfaction and see whether companies that score well on both grow faster.

To find out, I built a scatterplot and created a 2x2 plotting employee satisfaction against customer satisfaction.

  • I pulled employee ratings from

Glassdoor

using a scraper built with Python’s BeautifulSoup library.Customer ratings came from the

American Customer Satisfaction Index

. To see how fast the companies were growing, I sized them using 3-year revenue growth figures pulled from the

IEX Cloud

financial data API, using a script written in Python.

The example above shows supermarkets. I’ve done charts for sectors ranging from fast food to package delivery (US Post Office, anyone?). All tell a similar story. But which companies are most loved, least loved and getting the biggest financial payoffs is often a surprise.
